WordSnap — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 May 2026

WordSnap (“WordSnap”, “we”, “us”) is a vocabulary-learning service delivered as a Telegram bot (@WordSnapBot) and a Telegram Mini App. This policy explains what personal data we collect when you use WordSnap, why we use it, who we share it with, and the choices you have. By using WordSnap you agree to this policy.

1. Data we collect

  • Telegram account information. When you open the bot or Mini App, Telegram provides us your Telegram user ID, and may provide your username, first and last name, profile photo, language code, and (for Mini App launches) a start parameter such as a referral or campaign tag. We do not receive your phone number or contacts.
  • Learning data. Words and phrases you save (“snaps”), translations, example sentences, your review answers and timing, spaced-repetition schedule, streaks, XP, settings, and language preferences.
  • Content you submit. Text you send to the bot or enter in the app (for example a word to look up or a sentence to translate), and messages you send us for support.
  • Usage and device data. Pages and features you open, actions you take, session times, app version, approximate device/OS and browser type, and IP address — collected through our analytics and error-monitoring tools.
  • Subscription data. Your plan (free / Pro), trial status, and transaction metadata (amount, currency, timestamp, status). Payments are processed by Telegram and/or our payment provider — we never receive or store your card or bank details.

2. How we use your data

  • Provide and operate the service — store your words, run the spaced-repetition algorithm, send review reminders, sync your progress.
  • Generate translations, explanations and example sentences (see “AI features” below).
  • Process subscriptions, trials and payments.
  • Understand how the product is used and improve it (analytics, A/B tests).
  • Provide customer support and respond to your requests.
  • Keep the service secure, prevent abuse and fraud, and debug errors.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

Our legal bases (where the GDPR applies) are: performance of our contract with you (operating the app), our legitimate interests (improving and securing the service, analytics), your consent (where requested), and compliance with law.

3. AI features

Some features (translation, word explanations, example generation) send the text you submit to third-party AI providers to produce a response. We send only what is needed for the feature and do not knowingly include unrelated personal data. We choose providers that do not use submitted content to train their models for such API use, or where an opt-out applies.

4. Who we share data with

We do not sell your personal data. We share it only with service providers (processors) that help us run WordSnap, under appropriate contracts:

  • Telegram — the platform the bot and Mini App run on.
  • Cloud hosting — Vercel (front-end / Mini App) and Railway (back-end).
  • Database — managed PostgreSQL (Supabase).
  • Product analytics — PostHog (EU-hosted instance).
  • Error monitoring — Sentry.
  • AI providers — for the AI features described above.
  • Payment processor — Telegram and/or our payment partner, for billing.

We may also disclose data if required by law, or to protect our rights, users, or the public.

5. Data location and international transfers

Our servers and service providers are located in the European Union and the United States. Where data is transferred outside your country, we rely on appropriate safeguards such as the providers’ Standard Contractual Clauses.

6. Data retention

We keep your account and learning data for as long as your account is active. If you stop using WordSnap, or ask us to delete your data, we will delete or anonymise it within a reasonable period, except where we must keep certain records (for example payment records) to comply with the law. Aggregated, non-identifying analytics may be retained longer.

7. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, delete or export your personal data, to object to or restrict certain processing, and to withdraw consent. To exercise any of these rights, contact us (see below). You also have the right to complain to your local data protection authority.

8. Children

WordSnap is not directed to children under 13 (or under the minimum age in your country, e.g. 16 in parts of the EU). We do not knowingly collect data from such children. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it.

9. Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data (such as encrypted connections and access controls). No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will change the “Last updated” date above and, for material changes, notify you in the app or bot. Continued use of WordSnap after an update means you accept the revised policy.

11. Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Message us through @WordSnapBot on Telegram, or email zvu83@yahoo.com.
